Intelligent Inventory Optimization System

An State of the art machine learning-powered inventory optimization system that predicts optimal stock levels using time-series forecasting and machine learning, dynamically adjusting recommendations based on sales data and seasonal patterns to reduce both stockouts and overstock issues.

ARIMA, Prophet, and XGBoost models SQL and Azure Data Lake Airflow for pipeline orchestration

Business Problem

A national retail chain with 500+ stores and 10,000+ SKUs was experiencing $12M in annual losses due to inefficient inventory management. Store managers relied on manual ordering processes and basic rules of thumb, resulting in frequent stockouts of popular items (lost sales) and excessive inventory of slow-moving products (increased carrying costs and markdowns).

The company's challenges can be summarized as follows:

  • Inaccurate Demand Forecasting and Stockouts: Manual ordering processes led to frequent stockouts, resulting in lost sales and disappointed customers.
  • Excessive Inventory of Slow-Moving Products: Inefficient inventory management resulted in excessive inventory, increasing carrying costs and markdowns.
  • Inefficient Use of Resources and Labor: Manual processes diverted store managers' attention, increasing labor costs and reducing productivity.

Data Collection

Model Development

Built a three-tiered forecasting approach:

  • Base forecasting with ARIMA and Prophet for long-term trends
  • XGBoost for incorporating external variables and non-linear patterns
  • Ensemble method to combine predictions with dynamic weighting
