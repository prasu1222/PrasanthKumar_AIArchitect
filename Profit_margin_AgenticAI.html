Complex Cost Optimization Driving margin higher

An cutting-edge Agentic AI-powered solution leverages Agentic AI to deliver a comprehensive and actionable understanding of your financial performance, By providing a clear and concise picture of your profit and loss.

Business Problem

A leading manufacturing company faces the challenge of managing cost optimization across a diverse range of customized products catering to various industries, including agriculture, mining, defense, truck engine, and car engine. The complexity arises from the vast amount of data generated by multiple product lines, customer-specific requirements, and varying industry demands. As a result, the company struggles to maintain a clear understanding of its cost structure and identify opportunities for optimization.

The company's challenges can be summarized as follows:

  • Identifying parts that have increased in cost and their impact on customers
  • Determining areas where cost optimization opportunities exist
  • Understanding the effect of cost increases on margins

Business Value

Our solution enables your organization to unlock significant business value by optimizing costs, improving engineering efficiency, and informing pricing strategies.

Cost Optimization: Understand why costs are increasing for specific parts, and identify opportunities to reduce costs and improve margins by switching suppliers or negotiating better prices. resulting in $10M annual savings.

Engineering Optimization: Explore alternative parts or redesign products to reduce overall costs and improve margins.

Informed Price Increase: Develop a robust negotiation strategy to support price increases, driven by a clear understanding of cost increases and their impact on pricing.

System Architecture

Technical Implementation

1

Data Collection

We collected data from multiple data systems and ingested it into Azure, ensuring a unified and centralized data repository. This step enabled us to access and process large volumes of data from various sources.

2

Feature Engineering

We utilized the latest data engineering techniques to ensure that the data is refreshed regularly and created workflows to generate the feature layer of the dataset. This step involved designing and implementing data pipelines to extract, transform, and load data into a suitable format for modeling.

3

Agentic AI Model Development & Training

We used LangChain to develop individual agents that were later orchestrated into an Agentic system using LangGraph, leveraging the power of Agentic AI. This step involved designing and training multiple models to perform specific tasks and integrating them into a cohesive system.

4

Model Observatory

We implemented a model observability framework to monitor and track the performance of our Agentic AI models in real-time, enabling us to identify areas for improvement. This step involved setting up metrics and logging mechanisms to measure model performance and detect potential issues.

5

Deployment

We deployed our Agentic AI-powered solution to a cloud-based infrastructure, ensuring scalability, reliability, and high availability. This step involved configuring and deploying the solution to a production-ready environment, where it can be accessed and utilized by stakeholders.
